Revibe'22

On 28th May

Revibe is a National Level symposium organized by Students Guidance Cell (SGC). Technical events like coding and debugging and Web designing contests help students to showcase their technical talents. Non-Technical events like Cooking without fire, photography, connection and chess game are conducted to tranquil one's mind from this short semester, without them giving up or losing motivation. Revibe is Conducted for the past three years. Except 2021 .
