Cooking withoutfire

Topic: Cooking Without Fire

Registration fee: Rs 100/-

Timing :10 AM-12 PM

Venue: Auditorium

RULES AND REGULATIONS:
  • 1. Participants should not bring any pre-cooked items.

  • 2. Two members should be in a team to participate in the event

  • 3.Participants are supposed to display a chart having the name, ingredients and calorie count each< dish they have prepared.

  • 4.All the items required for cooking should be brought by the participants themselves, including water, Only working table will be provided.

  • 5.Participants should not bring any cut, chopped or grates raw materials. They should process it on the spot within allotted time.

  • 6.Participants can bring non-electric equipments like mixer, chopper, juicer, grater etc..

  • 7.The evaluation will be based on the taste of the dish, nutritional level, cleanliness and presentation, unique name of the dish
