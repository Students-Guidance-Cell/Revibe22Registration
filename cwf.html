Cooking withoutfire

Topic: Cooking Without Fire

Registration fee: Rs 100/-

Timing :10 AM - 12.30 PM

Venue: Auditorium

RULES AND REGULATIONS:
  • Participants should not bring any pre-cooked items.

  • Two members should be in a team to participate in the event

  • Participants are supposed to display a chart having the name, ingredients and calorie count each< dish they have prepared.

  • All the items required for cooking should be brought by the participants themselves, including water, Only working table will be provided.

  • Participants should not bring any cut, chopped or grates raw materials. They should process it on the spot within allotted time.

  • Participants can bring non-electric equipments like mixer, chopper, juicer, grater etc..

  • The evaluation will be based on the taste of the dish, nutritional level, cleanliness and presentation, unique name of the dish
